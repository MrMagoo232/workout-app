Need Help?

  • Click on the map to start plotting a route.
  • Each click adds a marker and draws a line.
  • Distance is automatically calculated.
  • Undo removes the last marker.
  • Cancel clears all points and closes controls.
  • Finish completes the route and opens the form.
  • Clicking finish brings up a short form.
  • Select your workout type (e.g. running or hiking).
  • Enter how long the activity took.
  • Enter your cadence if running or elevation gain (or loss such as -200) if hiking.
  • Press the enter key to save your route.
  • Find a previous route by clicked on any of your previous routes.

    Need Help? Click on the question mark on the top right side of the map to tips on using this site.
